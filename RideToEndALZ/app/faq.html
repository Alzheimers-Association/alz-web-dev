  • General FAQ:

  • what ride cycling route distance location
    Ride to End ALZ is a fully supported, one-day fundraising event for novice cyclists, weekend warriors and avid enthusiasts. Choose from multiple route options that challenge you to go the distance to fight Alzheimer's.
  • location city state route austin texas tx fort collins colorado co
    The Fall Virtual Ride Challenge will take place either indoors through the Zwift app on a trainer or an outdoor route of your choice on October 11th.
  • when date time austin texas tx fort collins colorado co
    Visit the homepage for in-person and/or virtual for dates and details.
  • age requirement year participate participation ride
    All participants must be 13 years of age to participate.
  • long route distance option miles
    Riders will be able to choose from multiple route options, ranging from 20 - 100 miles.
  • how register registration where
    To register, visit www.alz.org/ride and click "register".
  • fundraising package benefit
    There is no registration fee for the virtual challenge and fundraising for the virtual event is optional, but we encourage you to ask for donations to support you and your fight against Alzheimer's disease. For the in-person events, there is a $75 registration fee and $500 fundraising minimum.
  • cost register registration fee donation
    It is $75 to register for the in-person events. There is no registration fee to participate in the virtual challenge.
  • support fundraising training concierge service
    From the moment you register until you cross the finish line, we will support you with expert tips and coaching to reach your fundraising goal. Every participant will receive their own personal fundraising webpage in order to seek donations from your friends and family to reach the minimum.
  • cancel registration donation
    Donations are non-refundable and non-transferable. All donations go toward research efforts to discover methods of treatment and prevention for Alzheimer's disease. If you registered and fundraised for the 2020 Colorado event, due to Covid-19 you are able to transfer that registration/fundraising to either the TX event in the fall of 2020 or the Fort Collins, Colorado, event in the summer of 2021. Email ride@alz.org to confirm your transfer of funds.
  • pet allow dogs cats animals
    Sorry, no pets allowed.
